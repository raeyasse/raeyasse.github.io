Project Overview

  1. Application Description: A mountain summer camp website for Camp Rocky River that connects potential campers and their families with camp registration, inquiries, and a general overview of the camp.
  2. Mission: To provide a way for all youth to experience the outdoors. Through sponsors, the camp covers 75-100% of the cost for families enrolled in SNAP or EBT.
  3. Intended Users: Families with children ages 5-16 looking for overnight summer camps in the Appalachian Mountains.
  4. What Makes Camp Rocky River Stand Out:
    • Leadership development programs
    • Volunteer work throughout neighboring communities
    • Wilderness skills training
    • Activities include swimming, hiking, canoeing, ropes courses, arts and crafts, and gaga ball
  5. Content of the Website:
    • Summer camp schedules
    • Photos
    • Frequently asked questions
    • Ways to schedule a tour of the facilities

Dynamic Functionality

  1. Enrollment Form with Validation (Enrollment Page): A registration form will collect camper and family information including name, email, and phone number. JavaScript will validate all fields before submission with a confirmation message upon success.
    • Why: Ensures accurate data is collected from families signing up for camp, tours, or the mailing list.
    • Example: kramden.org
  2. Image Slideshow/Gallery (Activities Page): An interactive photo gallery will display camp activity photos with previous/next buttons powered by JavaScript.
    • Why: Visual content helps families see what activities are offered, making the camp more appealing.
    • Example: camptecumseh.org
  3. jQuery-UI Accordion (Our Mission Page): The mission, values, and sponsor information will be organized into a jQuery-UI accordion, allowing users to expand and collapse each section.
    • Why: Breaks up dense information into digestible sections without overwhelming the user.
    • Example: jqueryui.com/accordion
  4. AJAX Content Loading (Testimonials Page): Testimonial data will be loaded dynamically via AJAX from an external JSON file without a full page reload.
    • Why: Improves page performance and provides a smoother experience when browsing reviews.
    • Example: yelp.com
