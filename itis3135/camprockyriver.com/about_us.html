About us

Camp Rocky River has provided outdoor experiences since 1987, helping kids grow and discover their potential, with sponsors covering 75 to 100% of costs for SNAP or EBT families.

Camp Values

Inclusivity

Every child is welcome at Camp Rocky River. We work hard to ensure that financial barriers never stand between a child and the outdoors.

Leadership

We develop confident, compassionate leaders through hands-on programs that challenge campers to grow beyond their comfort zones.

Community

From neighboring towns to our camp family, we are committed to giving back through volunteer work and community service.

Stewardship

We teach campers to respect and care for the natural world around them, building a lifelong love of the outdoors.

Safety and Policies

Staff Certifications

All staff are background checked, CPR certified, and trained in wilderness first aid before the start of each season.

Camper to Staff Ratio

We maintain a 5:1 camper to staff ratio at all times to ensure every child receives the attention and supervision they deserve.

Medical Policies

A licensed nurse is on site throughout every session. All campers must submit a health form and emergency contact information prior to arrival.

Code of Conduct

Camp Rocky River is a safe, respectful environment for all. Bullying, harassment, and discrimination of any kind are not tolerated.
