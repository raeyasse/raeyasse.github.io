Welcome to Camp Rocky River

Adventure for Every Child

Our Mission

At Camp Rocky River, we believe every child deserves the chance to experience the outdoors. Nestled in the Appalachian Mountains, our camp offers swimming, hiking, canoeing, ropes courses, arts and crafts, and more. Through generous sponsors, we cover 75–100% of costs for families enrolled in SNAP or EBT.

What Makes Us Different

Leadership Development

Campers build confidence and teamwork through guided leadership programs.

Community Service

We give back through volunteer work in neighboring Appalachian communities.

Wilderness Skills

From fire building to navigation, campers learn real outdoor survival skills.

Accessible to All

No family should be turned away. Sponsorships make camp possible for everyone.
