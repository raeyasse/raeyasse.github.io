Activities

From mountain trails to campfire nights, every day at Camp Rocky River is packed with adventure, teamwork, and memories that last a lifetime. Here's a look at what campers experience during their stay.

Activity Types

Hiking

Campers explore the breathtaking trails of the Appalachian Mountains with experienced guides. From beginner-friendly nature walks to more challenging summit hikes, there is a trail for every skill level.

Team Building

Through ropes courses, trust exercises, and group challenges, campers develop communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills. Our team building programs are at the heart of what makes Camp Rocky River special.

Outdoor Games

From swimming and canoeing to arts and crafts and gaga ball, campers stay active and engaged all day long. Our outdoor games are designed to be inclusive and fun for all ages and skill levels.

Campfire Events

Every evening winds down with campfire gatherings where campers share stories, sing songs, and build lasting friendships. These nightly traditions are a favorite memory for campers year after year.
