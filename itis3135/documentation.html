Getting Started

The Ambitious Rabbit Guild System is designed to manage explorers, quests, and resources.

Each explorer must register before accessing guild features and missions.

To begin, initialize your explorer profile:

createExplorer("YourName")

Once registered, you can begin accepting quests and exploring new environments.

Guild Roles

Each member must choose a role that defines their abilities and responsibilities.

Roles help organize teams and improve mission success rates.

  • Scout
  • Navigator
  • Treasure Hunter
  • Engineer
  • Strategist

To assign a role, use the system command:

assignRole("Scout")
Equipment

Equipment is required for all missions and must be properly managed.

Each item serves a specific purpose during exploration.

  • Lantern
  • Compass
  • Grappling Hook
  • Rocket Carrot

To equip an item, use:

equip("Lantern")

Always check your inventory before leaving for a quest.

Quest System

The quest system organizes all missions within the guild.

Each quest has a difficulty level and specific objectives.

  1. Select a quest
  2. Prepare equipment
  3. Begin mission
  4. Complete objectives
  5. Return safely

To start a quest:

startQuest("Beginner")

To complete a quest:

completeQuest()
Safety Rules

Safety is essential for all guild operations.

Ignoring safety protocols may result in mission failure.

  • Never explore alone
  • Carry proper equipment
  • Stay alert
  • Communicate with your team

To check your current status:

checkStatus()

Prepared explorers are successful explorers.
