Website Evaluations (CRAP Principles)

Commet Browser
  • Contrast: This page has excellent contrast with lighter values on the background and dark text that makes it very easy to identify and read
  • Repetition: The fonts, colors and shapes are repeated uniformly across the website
  • Alignment: All the elements are lined up and organized. Images and text don't touch the borders of the page and are aligned to fit the center
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together while seperate elements have enough space in between to communicate

Roadside America
  • Contrast: This site has lighter green background with a dark green text, making it harder to read at first glance. Which is a poor contrasting choice
  • Repetition: Fonts and colors aren't entirely uniform, different color backgrounds and fonts that chnage to bold towards the end of a sentence.
  • Alignment: The elements are aligned and organized.
  • Proximity: All the elements are crammed together while lacking some breathing room.
